Parts Warranty Information

Your guide to our parts and accessories warranty policy.

verified_user Warranty Coverage

AutoHaus provides a comprehensive warranty on all genuine parts and accessories purchased and installed at our dealership. This warranty covers defects in materials or workmanship under normal use and service. The warranty period is 12 months or 12,000 miles, whichever comes first, from the date of purchase. Please retain your original purchase receipt as proof of purchase.

check_circle What is Covered

Our warranty covers the replacement or repair of any part or accessory that proves to be defective in material or workmanship under normal use during the warranty period. This includes parts such as engines, transmissions, brakes, electrical components, and accessories like roof racks, floor mats, and electronic devices.

cancel What is Not Covered

The warranty does not cover damage from misuse, accidents, improper installation, or non-genuine parts. Normal wear and tear items like brake pads and tires are excluded, unless defective. Consequential damages, such as loss of vehicle use, are also not covered.

build Making a Warranty Claim

To make a warranty claim, please bring your vehicle to AutoHaus with the original purchase receipt. Our service department will inspect the part or accessory and determine if the defect is covered under warranty. If the claim is approved, we will repair or replace the part or accessory free of charge. If the claim is denied, we will provide a detailed explanation of the reasons for the denial.
